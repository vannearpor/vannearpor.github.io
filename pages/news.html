World News

Russian President Vladimir Putin Decides On National Cryptocurrency
Russian governments officials have gone through lengthy discussion regarding Bitcoin regulation and regulation of other cryptocurrencies. While seeing its potential, they're considering their own Russian cryptocurrency.
Omisego and Thai Central Bank in Financial Discussions
Ethereum co-founder Vitalik Buterin met with the Central Bank of Thailand to discuss how integration of digital currency utilizing blockchain-based serviced would further improved the country's banking systems and financial platforms.
Sweden Seeking Cashless Society Via Cryptocurrency
Sweden's central banks are considering introducing cryptocurrency due to the benefits of a cashless society.
